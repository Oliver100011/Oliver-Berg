The Sport

Lacrosse is one of the oldest team sports in North America, originally played by Indigenous peoples. Today it's one of the fastest-growing sports in the country — combining the physicality of hockey, the endurance of soccer, and the precision of basketball.

Players use a long-handled stick called a crosse to carry, pass, and shoot a small rubber ball into the opponent's goal. The game demands elite hand-eye coordination, explosive speed, and seamless team communication.
